Frequently asked questions about 360 feedback surveys

FAQ 1 | Question | What is a 360-degree feedback survey? | | Answer | A 360-degree feedback survey collects anonymous feedback from multiple perspectives — typically the leader being assessed (self), their manager, peers, and direct reports. FAQ 3 | Question | Can I customize the questions in a 360 survey? | | Answer | Yes. Every Launch 360 survey is fully customizable. FAQ 4 | Question | How long does it take to complete a 360 survey? | | Answer | Most raters complete a 360 survey in 10-15 minutes. Our surveys are designed to be comprehensive but not burdensome, with clear questions and intuitive rating scales. |
FAQ 5 | Question | Is 360 feedback confidential? | | Answer | Yes. By default, individual rater responses are anonymous. Only aggregated data is shown in reports. This encourages honest, constructive feedback. |
FAQ 6 | Question | Do I need certification to use Launch 360 surveys? | | Answer | No. Launch 360 has no certification requirement — unlike CCL's Skillscope or The Leadership Circle. Create an account and start your first assessment immediately. |

Section 2: What It Is
Element
Content
Headline
A strengths-based assessment rooted in positive psychology
Description
The Personal Impact Survey invites raters to share stories and examples of when the leader demonstrated exceptional leadership, impact, or influence. Rather than rating competencies on a scale, raters describe real moments of excellence.
Key Point 1
Narrative feedback, not just scores and charts
Key Point 2
Builds confidence by highlighting authentic strengths
Key Point 3
Identifies themes across multiple raters' responses
Key Point 4
Perfect complement to traditional 360 assessments
