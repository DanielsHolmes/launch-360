Most 360s focus on gaps. The Personal Impact Survey captures when leaders shine — through the eyes of those who know them.

Rooted in positive psychology, this unique assessment invites raters to share stories and examples of exceptional leadership. The result is a narrative report that builds confidence and authenticity — not a list of deficiencies.
